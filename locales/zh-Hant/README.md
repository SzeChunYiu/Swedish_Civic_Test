# Traditional Chinese Locale Workspace

Locale: Traditional Chinese (`zh-Hant`) for Traditional Chinese readers preparing for Swedish civic/citizenship study material.

Status: phase-1 glossary/phrasebook workspace. No Traditional Chinese app UI or question content is enabled yet; `zh-Hant` must remain `available: false` until the readiness ledger is complete.

Use these files before writing Traditional Chinese app copy:

- `glossary.md` — sourced civic and UI term decisions.
- `phrasebook.md` — candidate Traditional Chinese UI phrases for common study actions, feedback, source labels, and safe encouragement.
- `audit.md` — checked decisions and remaining release blockers.
- `docs/localization/sample-corpus/zh-Hant/style-guide.md` — variant boundary, mechanical-conversion repairs, culture/humor rules, and word-by-word audit checklist.

Default to Taiwan-style Traditional Chinese unless the product explicitly creates a Hong Kong-specific variant. `zh-Hant` must be native Traditional Chinese copy, not a script conversion from Simplified Chinese, and Swedish institutions must not be replaced with PRC/Taiwan/HK analogies.
