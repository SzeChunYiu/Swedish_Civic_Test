# Central Kurdish / Sorani Glossary

Status: phase-1 glossary. Use with `docs/localization/sample-corpus/ckb/style-guide.md`.

## Source notes

This phase uses Sorani Swedish public-service/education sources for register and Swedish/English official sources for Swedish civic facts. Useful Sorani register patterns include:

- learner/education wording from Skolverket SFI material: `خوێندکار`, `فێربوون`, `خوێندن`, `زانیاری`, `کۆرس`, `هەڵسەنگاندن`
- public-service/support wording from municipal/organization material: `یارمەتی`, `پشتیوانی`, `خزمەتگوزاری`, `پەیوەندی`, `ڕێکخستن`
- condition/action patterns such as `ئەگەر ... دەتوانیت ...`
- examples introduced with `بۆ نموونە`

Until a full Sorani Swedish civic source is found, mark Swedish civic terms as phase-1 candidates and keep Swedish terms in Latin script where learners must recognize them.

## False friends and style warnings

- Do not mix Sorani with Kurmanji vocabulary.
- Keep Arabic-based Kurdish script and right-to-left layout in mind.
- Do not replace Swedish `Riksdag`, `kommun`, or `region` with Kurdish/Iraqi/Iranian institutions.
- Avoid shaming feedback such as `تۆ هەڵەیت`.
- Do not joke about asylum, migration status, citizenship, passports, religion, gender, family, or exams.
- Legal/civic terms require native/legal review before release.
