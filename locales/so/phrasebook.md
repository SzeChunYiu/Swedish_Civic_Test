# Somali UI Phrasebook

Status: phase-1 candidate copy, not wired into the app. Keep `so` disabled until all release blockers in `audit.md` and `docs/localization/readiness.json` are resolved.

## Study actions

| UI function | Somali candidate | Avoid | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Start practice | Bilow tababarka | Samee bilow tababar | Natural verb-object CTA. |
| Continue practice | Sii wad tababarka | Ku sii soco session-ka | Avoid English `session`. |
| Try again | Mar kale isku day | Dib u isku day | Natural learner phrase. |
| Next question | Su’aasha xigta | Su’aal kale oo next ah | Keep Somali. |
| Previous question | Su’aashii hore | Su’aal hore previous | Keep Somali. |
| Submit answer | Gudbi jawaabta | Soo dir response | Avoid English loan. |
| Review explanation | Akhri sharaxaadda | Review samee sharaxaad | Natural Somali. |
| Review mistakes | Dib u eeg su’aalihii aad qalday | Review khaladaadka | Clear learner phrase. |
| Start mock exam | Bilow imtixaanka tijaabada ah | Bilow imtixaan fake ah | Do not imply official exam. |
