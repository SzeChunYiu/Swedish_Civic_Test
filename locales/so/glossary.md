# Somali Glossary

Status: phase-1 glossary. Use with `docs/localization/sample-corpus/so/style-guide.md`.

## Source notes

Informationsverige Somali pages provide strong Swedish-official Somali register for this app. Useful patterns include:

- `Ku saabsan Iswiidhan` for About Sweden.
- `Halkan waxaad ka akhrin kartaa...` for public-information navigation.
- `xuquuqda iyo waajibaadka` for rights and obligations.
- `takoorid` for discrimination.
- `hay’adaha dawlada` for government/public authorities.
- `sharciga deganaanshaha` for residence permit in newcomer context.

Keep Swedish civic facts anchored to Swedish official sources. Do not import Somalia-specific institutions into Swedish civics.

## False friends and style warnings

- Do not write Somali with English noun-stack order.
- Avoid replacing Swedish bodies with Somali political bodies.
- Do not overuse loanwords if a clear Somali public-information phrase exists.
- Keep tone respectful; avoid jokes about migration, religion, family, accents, or legal status.
