# Tigrinya Glossary

Status: phase-1 glossary. Use with `docs/localization/sample-corpus/ti/style-guide.md`.

## Source notes

This phase uses Tigrinya official Swedish newcomer/civic pages from Informationsverige for register and Swedish civic facts. Useful source-backed patterns include:

- public-information framing such as `ብዛዕባ ሽወደን` and `እዚ ጽሑፍ ብዛዕባ ... እዩ።`
- rights and participation language such as `መሰላት`, `መሰል ኣለካ`, `ናጻን ፍትሓውን ምርጫ`, and `ናጽነት ሓሳብካ ምግላጽ`
- governance language from the Sweden-is-governed page, including `ቅዋም`, `መሰረታውያን ሕግታት`, `ሃገራዊ ባይቶ`, and `መንግስቲ`
- sensitive rights language such as `ኣድልዎ`, `ሕጊ ኣንጻር ኣድልዎ`, and `ተሓላቕ ፍትሒ ኣንጻር ኣድልዎ (DO)`

Do not mechanically translate Swedish compounds. Keep Swedish terms such as `Riksdag`, `kommun`, and agency names visible on first mention when the learner needs to recognize the Swedish system.

## False friends and style warnings

- Do not translate Tigrinya by copying Swedish/English word order.
- Do not replace `Riksdag`, `kommun`, or Swedish agencies with Eritrean/Ethiopian institutions.
- Avoid shaming learner feedback such as direct `ጌጋ ኢኻ`.
- Ge'ez script text expansion, punctuation, and line breaks need runtime review on mobile.
- Legal/civic terms such as citizenship, residence permit, and application need native/legal review before release.
- Avoid jokes about migration, citizenship, religion, gender, family, nationality, or accents.
