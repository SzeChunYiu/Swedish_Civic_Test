# Polish Glossary

Status: phase-1 glossary. Use with `docs/localization/sample-corpus/pl/style-guide.md`.

## Source notes

Swedish-official Polish civic pages are limited, so this phase uses Polish public-service sources for register and Swedish/English official sources for Swedish facts. Useful Polish public-service patterns include:

- `zezwolenie na pobyt czasowy`, `zezwolenie na pobyt stały`, `wniosek`, `złożyć wniosek`, `decyzja`, and `dokument podróży` from Polish government migration pages.
- `prawa i obowiązki` for rights and obligations.
- `cudzoziemiec` for foreigner in Polish legal register, but avoid using it as a learner label unless the context is legal.
- `organ władzy publicznej`, `urząd`, and `organ administracji` for public bodies by context.

Use Swedish/English official sources for Sweden-specific terms. Do not map Swedish institutions to Polish institutions such as Sejm, województwo, or gmina unless explicitly explaining an analogy.

## False friends and style warnings

- Do not translate `Riksdag` as `Sejm`.
- Do not translate `kommun` mechanically as Polish `gmina` when the Swedish institution is being taught.
- Avoid English calques such as `zaaplikuj o obywatelstwo`; use `ubiegać się o obywatelstwo` or `złożyć wniosek`.
- Polish inflection matters; placeholders must be tested in full sentences.
- Keep tone respectful and plain; avoid jokes about migration, bureaucracy, nationality, or accents.
