# Simplified Chinese Glossary

## Research Notes

Locale: Simplified Chinese for Mainland Chinese readers preparing for Swedish
citizenship-study material.

Register: use Mainland Chinese public-information style: formal, concise, and
plain. Prefer terms that an educated Mainland Chinese reader would expect in
government, legal-rights, migration, and civic-education copy. Do not map
Swedish institutions onto PRC institutions. Keep Swedish institutional names
where the Swedish name is part of the official English/Swedish usage, and add a
clear Chinese gloss when needed.

Regional language choices:

- Use Mainland vocabulary and punctuation: 政府, 议会, 公民身份, 居留许可,
  信息, 网络, 软件, 劳动力市场, 市镇, 省/省级行政区 only when the Swedish
  concept truly maps to it.
- Avoid Taiwan/Hong Kong terms such as 資訊, 網路, 軟體, 列印, 立法院, 行政院,
  and avoid Traditional punctuation conventions.
- For Swedish governance levels, use Sweden-specific explanations instead of
  PRC administrative analogies. `kommun` is 市镇, not China's "city";
  `region` is 大区/地区一级自治机构 by context, not automatically 省.

Authoritative terminology pass:

- Sweden Abroad Chinese pages for Mainland China use 瑞典驻华大使馆,
  瑞典移民局, 居留许可, 签证, 在瑞典工作, and Chinese public-service phrasing
  aimed at Chinese users.
- Government.se describes Sweden's national executive as the Government and
  the Government Offices as the body that assists the Government and ministers.
- Government.se explains Sweden has national, regional, and local levels of
  government; the people are represented nationally by the Riksdag, and
  municipalities and regional councils are elected locally/regionally.
- The Swedish Migration Agency states that people can become Swedish citizens
  by application or notification and uses "Swedish citizenship" as the core
  official English term.
- The Parliamentary Ombudsmen (JO) English site states that JO are appointed by
  the Riksdag and supervise that public authorities comply with laws and other
  statutes.
- The Equality Ombudsman (DO) English pages use Equality Ombudsman,
  Discrimination Act, equal rights and opportunities, and discrimination as
  official terms.
- UN/OHCHR Chinese treaty usage supports 儿童权利公约 for the Convention on
  the Rights of the Child.

## False Friends And Style Decisions

- `stat` is often 国家 or 国家层级, not automatically 政府.
- `kommun` is a Swedish municipality; do not translate it as China-style
  市/区/县.
- `region` in Swedish civics means the elected regional level, not just a
  geographic area.
- `allmän` may mean public, general, or ordinary depending on context; do not
  mechanically translate it as 普通 in legal terms.
- Keep Swedish proper nouns with Swedish spelling when they are place or
  institution names: Vänern, Vättern, Mälaren, Öland, Östersjön, Skåne,
  Riksdag.
- Use Mainland punctuation and spacing conventions for Chinese prose, while
  preserving Swedish acronyms and source references exactly.
