# Persian/Farsi Glossary

Status: phase-1 glossary. Use with `docs/localization/sample-corpus/fa/style-guide.md`.

## Source notes

Informationsverige Persian pages provide Swedish-official Persian register for this app. Useful patterns include:

- `درباره سوئد` for About Sweden.
- `جهت یابی در جامعه` and `آشنائی با جامعه سوئد` for civic-orientation context.
- `حق و حقوق و وظایف` for rights and duties.
- `اجازه اقامت` for residence permit.
- `تبعیض` for discrimination.
- `آزادی دین و مذهب` for freedom of religion.

Keep Swedish civic facts anchored to Swedish official sources. Do not map Swedish institutions to Iran/Afghanistan/Tajikistan-specific institutions.

## False friends and style warnings

- Do not mix Dari/Iranian/Tajik variants accidentally; choose the target register before release.
- Do not translate Swedish institutions into Iran/Afghanistan-specific bodies.
- Avoid English noun-stack order.
- Use respectful learner tone; avoid sarcasm after wrong answers.
- RTL runtime and accessibility review is mandatory before enabling Persian.
