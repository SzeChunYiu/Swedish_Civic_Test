# Google Play listing — Sweden Citizenship Test Prep

## App name

Sweden Citizenship Test Prep

## Short description

UHR-based Swedish civic practice with mock exams and progress tracking.

## Full description

Sweden Citizenship Test Prep helps you study Swedish civic knowledge in small daily sessions.

Features:

- UHR-referenced practice questions.
- Swedish question text with English support.
- Explanations and source references after practice answers.
- Chapter list covering the UHR education material.
- Timed mock exam mode with no ads during exam sessions.
- Local progress tracking, mistakes, XP, streaks, weak chapters, and badges.
- Optional Swedish audio for questions and answer choices.
- Privacy-first MVP: no account required and progress stored locally on your device.

Important: This app is not official and is not affiliated with UHR, Skolverket, Migrationsverket, or the Swedish government. Practice questions are created for learning and are not real exam questions. Always use the official UHR education material as your primary source.
