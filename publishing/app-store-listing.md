## Description

Almost Swedish is an independent study companion for people preparing for the Swedish civic knowledge part of the citizenship test.

Study in short daily sessions with:

- UHR-referenced practice questions.
- Swedish questions with English support.
- Explanations after practice answers.
- Chapter browsing across the UHR education material.
- Mistake review and weak-topic tracking.
- Timed mock exams with no ads during the exam.
- Local progress, XP, streaks, and badges stored locally on your device.
- Optional Swedish text-to-speech for questions and answer options.

Almost Swedish is ad-supported on study screens. The release uses Google Mobile
Ads / AdMob, App Tracking Transparency, and Google UMP consent for ad delivery
and consent handling. Remove Ads is an optional one-time **29 SEK** in-app
purchase that removes ads only. The full question bank remains available
without purchase.

This app is not official and is not affiliated with UHR, Skolverket, Migrationsverket, or the Swedish government. Practice questions are created for learning and are not real exam questions. Always study the UHR education material directly.
