Almost Swedish privacy policy

Almost Swedish is an independent study app. It is not official and is not affiliated with UHR, Skolverket, Migrationsverket, or the Swedish government.

No account required

The Almost Swedish v1.0 app requires no account, email address, phone number, or profile registration.

Local study progress

Study progress, settings, mistakes, XP, streaks, badges, bookmarks, and audio preferences are stored locally on the device.

Current data collection and sharing posture

The app itself still has no account database, remote progress sync, analytics SDK, crash reporting SDK, or support form. Because the v1.0 native build includes Google Mobile Ads, App Tracking Transparency, Google UMP consent, and a Remove Ads in-app purchase, the store privacy posture is that data may be collected or shared by Google Mobile Ads, the app stores, or consent systems for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, purchase handling, and app functionality.

Ads and purchases

Free study screens may show Google Mobile Ads or AdMob placements when real ads are enabled. Timed mock exam screens stay ad-free. Remove Ads is an optional one-time non-consumable in-app purchase for 29 SEK that removes ads from study screens and can be restored through the app store. For restore or revalidation, the app may keep a local purchase-status record with the store product ID, purchase token or transaction ID, and receipt-validation timestamp.

On iOS, tracking-based advertising must wait for App Tracking Transparency authorization. Where required, Google UMP consent is collected before real ad serving. If analytics, crash reporting, accounts, remote progress sync, or support collection are enabled later, this privacy policy, Apple privacy labels, Google Play Data safety answers, and in-app privacy copy must be reviewed and updated before submission.
