# 13 — Legal and Compliance Notes

This is not legal advice. Confirm important questions with a lawyer before commercial launch.

## Official affiliation risk

The app must not look like an official government app.

Do not use:

- UHR logo,
- Skolverket logo,
- Migrationsverket logo,
- Swedish government branding,
- official-looking seals,
- wording such as "official app",
- wording such as "approved by UHR".

Use:

> Independent study tool.

## Disclaimer

Use this on onboarding, about page, mock exam start, and store listing:

> This app is an independent study tool for the Swedish citizenship civic test. It is not official and is not affiliated with UHR, Skolverket, Migrationsverket, or the Swedish government. Practice questions are created for learning purposes and are not real exam questions.

## Copyright

Risk:

- The UHR PDF is public, but public availability does not automatically allow full commercial republication.
- Images in official documents may have third-party rights.
- Full-copying the PDF into the app could create legal risk.

Safer approach:

- create original summaries,
- create original questions,
- quote only short excerpts,
- provide source references,
- link to official UHR material,
- avoid images from the PDF,
- avoid duplicating the PDF's full text.

## Question wording

Do not claim questions are real exam questions.

Use:

- practice questions,
- exam-style questions,
- study questions.

Avoid:

- real test questions,
- leaked questions,
- official questions.

## Google Ads / AdMob risk

Avoid misleading ads or store listings that suggest users can apply for citizenship through the app.

Safe wording:

- prepare for the civic test,
- study Swedish society,
- independent practice app.

Avoid:

- get Swedish citizenship,
- apply here,
- guaranteed citizenship,
- official citizenship service.

## Privacy

Avoid collecting sensitive immigration data.

Do not ask for:

- personal identity number,
- case number,
- citizenship application status,
- migration history,
- passport data,
- residence permit details.

If accounts are added later, collect only:

- email,
- username/display name optional,
- progress data.

## Children and age

This is mainly for adults, but teenagers may use it.

Avoid:

- social features,
- user-generated content,
- targeted sensitive ads,
- collecting unnecessary personal data.

## Accessibility

Support:

- readable font sizes,
- screen readers,
- good contrast,
- audio controls,
- no time pressure outside exam mode.

## Store compliance notes

### Apple

Apple review focuses on safety, performance, business, design, and legal requirements.

Make sure:

- app is stable,
- app does what listing says,
- purchases are clear,
- subscriptions are not deceptive,
- privacy labels are accurate,
- no misleading official claims.

### Google Play

Google Play requires accurate Data Safety declarations.

Make sure:

- declare ad SDK data usage,
- declare analytics/crash data,
- provide privacy policy,
- avoid misleading metadata,
- comply with educational content claims.
