## Source usage policy inside app

Every answer reference should show:

- source publisher,
- document title,
- chapter,
- section,
- page if available,
- short quote or paraphrase,
- URL to official material.

Example:

> Source: UHR, `Sverige i fokus`, chapter `Landet Sverige`, section `Geografi, klimat och natur`, p. 5.

## Citation rules for app content

- Use short quotes only.
- Do not reproduce full pages.
- Do not copy UHR images.
- Do not use UHR logo.
- Do not imply endorsement.
- Link users to the original UHR material.
