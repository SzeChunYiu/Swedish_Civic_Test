1. lib/motion/useReducedMotion.ts — hook returning current reduce-motion preference, subscribes to AccessibilityInfo changes.
2. lib/motion/useFadeIn.ts — fade-in Animated.Value driver respecting reduce-motion (instant when on).
3. lib/motion/useSpringPress.ts — scale-down spring for pressable elements.
4. lib/motion/useProgress.ts — animated numeric progress (0→target), reduce-motion fallback.
5. lib/a11y/announce.ts — wrapper around AccessibilityInfo.announceForAccessibility with debounce.
6. lib/a11y/focusOrder.ts — helpers for accessibilityElementsHidden / importantForAccessibility on overlay patterns.
7. lib/a11y/contrast.ts — pure function: WCAG ratio from two colors. Export `assertAA(fg, bg)` for dev-time use.
8. lib/motion/haptics.ts — semantic wrapper over expo-haptics: tapLight, correct, incorrect.
9. lib/motion/index.ts + lib/a11y/index.ts — barrel exports.

Tokens (lib/theme/motion.ts) must exist (DESIGN-TOKENS task 6) before tasks 1-4. Task 5-8 can start immediately.
