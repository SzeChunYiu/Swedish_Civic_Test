## Defects

- `q502`, `q503`: NATO/EU true/false prompts publish answer fragments
  (`Nästan samtidigt ansöka...` / `Apply...`, `Lämna...` / `Leave...`) rather
  than complete propositions.
- `q506`, `q507`, `q518`, and `q519`: generated true/false prompts use
  meta-statement wording (`Det stämmer i sak att` / `It is factually true
  that`, `Det stämmer inte att` / `It is not true that`) and expose awkward
  Swedish capitalization after `att`.
- `q514`, `q515`, `q522`, `q523`, `q526`, `q527`, `q546`, and `q547`: generated
  true/false prompts are bare answer labels or term names, not full
  learner-facing statements.
- `q530`, `q531`, `q542`, `q543`, and `q550`: generated true/false prompts are
  activity/list fragments instead of complete propositions.
- `q504`, `q505`, `q508`, `q512`, `q516`, `q517`, `q520`, `q524`, `q528`,
  `q532`, `q536`, `q540`, `q544`, and `q548`: generated single-choice variants
  include the already-routed fallback option `Det går inte att avgöra av
  materialet` / `It cannot be determined from the material`. This belongs to
  `REVIEWER-GENERATED-UNKNOWN-MATERIAL-OPTION-1`.
