# VERIFY q351-q400 audit - 2026-05-18

Scope: generated published variants `q351` through `q400` in
`data/questions.ts` / `data/additionalQuestions.ts`, derived from source
questions `q052` through the first generated variant of `q065`, on current
`origin/main` commit `711b20f`.

Primary source opened: UHR, `Sverige i fokus`, official PDF:
https://www.uhr.se/globalassets/_uhr.se/medborgarskapsprovet/utbildningsmaterial/sverige-i-fokus.pdf

Result: facts, citations, correct answers, and distractors are supported for
the slice. Eighteen generated true/false rows still fail the natural-language
bar and are mirrored in `site/questions.js`, so the defect belongs to
DATA-INTEGRITY rather than a hand edit to exported rows.

## Defects

- `q357`, `q358`, `q361`, `q362`, `q381`, `q382`: true/false prompts built
  from `Vad gäller för...` questions publish stiff `För... gäller att` /
  `For...` splice phrasing. The assertions are supported, but the learner-
  visible Swedish and English are not natural quiz prose.
- `q365`, `q366`, `q393`, `q394`: year and percentage answers are published
  as bare true/false prompts (`2020`, `1979`, `Cirka 70 procent`, `Cirka
  30 procent`) without a complete proposition.
- `q373`, `q374`: national-minority true/false prompts are bare lists rather
  than complete statements saying these are, or are not, Sweden's five
  national minorities.
- `q377`, `q378`, `q385`, `q386`: answer fragments beginning with infinitive
  verbs are published as full true/false prompts (`Representera...`,
  `Besluta...`, `Arbeta...`, `Se till...` / `Represent...`, `Decide...`,
  `Work...`, `Make sure...`). The facts and distractors are supported, but
  the prompt grammar is not natural.
- `q389`, `q390`: public-sector true/false prompts are noun phrases without
  a predicate. They need generated wording that states that those activities
  are, or are not, the public sector.
