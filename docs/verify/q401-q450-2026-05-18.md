## Defects

- `q401`, `q402`: wage-setting true/false prompts are answer fragments
  (`Genom...` / `Through...`) rather than complete propositions.
- `q405`, `q406`, `q445`, `q446`: reason prompts splice answer fragments after
  `En anledning är att` / `One reason is that`. The English is ungrammatical,
  and `q446` also publishes `eU-medlemskapet`.
- `q410`, `q414`, `q429`, `q430`: false-statement prompts are predicate-less
  noun or infinitive fragments, not natural true/false statements.
- `q425`, `q426`, `q433`, `q434`: generated list prompts publish welfare
  examples as bare lists without a predicate.
- `q437`, `q438`: generated true/false prompts use meta scaffolding
  (`Det stämmer...` / `It is...`) instead of direct learner-facing statements;
  `q438` also creates a confusing negative assertion.
