# VERIFY q301-q350 audit - 2026-05-18

Scope: generated published variants `q301` through `q350` in
`data/questions.ts` / `data/additionalQuestions.ts`, derived from source
questions `q040` through `q052`, on current `origin/main` commit `170b89d`.

Primary source opened: UHR, `Sverige i fokus`, official PDF:
https://www.uhr.se/globalassets/_uhr.se/medborgarskapsprovet/utbildningsmaterial/sverige-i-fokus.pdf

Result: facts, citations, correct answers, and distractors are supported for
the slice. Eighteen generated true/false rows still fail the natural-language
bar and are mirrored in `site/questions.js`, so the defect belongs to
DATA-INTEGRITY rather than a hand edit to exported rows.

## Defects

- `q305`, `q306`: the legal-certainty true/false prompts append the source
  question after a complete answer fragment: `...överklaga en dom beskriver...`
  / `...appeal a judgment describes...` and the same pattern for the false
  government-or-Riksdag option. The facts and distractor are supported, but
  the visible generated stems are not natural Swedish or English.
- `q313`, `q314`, `q321`, `q322`: the police and free-media variants use
  infinitive or option fragments before `beskriver` / `describes`, producing
  prompts such as `Att besluta statens budget beskriver...` and `They should
  replace political elections describes...`.
- `q317`, `q318`: the English criminal-responsibility prompts drop the verb
  before `criminally responsible`, leaving ungrammatical stems even though the
  Swedish text and answer keys are supported.
- `q325`, `q326`: the public-access variants turn `Genom att...` / `By...`
  answer fragments into standalone true/false prompts instead of complete
  propositions.
- `q329`, `q330`, `q337`, `q338`: statement-derived true/false prompts publish
  meta wording such as `Det stämmer i sak att` / `It is factually true that`
  with a capitalized full statement after `att`, which reads like generator
  scaffolding rather than learner-facing prose.
- `q333`, `q334`: the public-service true/false variants publish bare lists of
  organizations without a predicate that says they are, or are not, public
  service companies.
- `q345`, `q346`: the English UN-purpose variants publish `One reason is that
  prevent...` and `One reason is that decide...`; the intended facts and
  distractors are supported, but the generated English is not grammatical.
