# Competitive Teardown — Sweden Citizenship Test Prep

_Researched 2026-05-17. Benchmarks: Duolingo, Quizlet, AnkiDroid/FSRS, the Official Life in the UK Test app (TSO) + BritPass, German Einbürgerungstest apps (Panterra / Migrando "Buerger-Test"), Swedish körkort apps (iKörkort, Körkortonline, Körkort AI). Every recommendation below names the source app it is drawn from. Screen names refer to the real Expo Router files in this repo._

## (a) Executive summary

1. **Our monetization is already best-in-class for this category — protect it.** iKörkort's headline selling point is literally "pay once, no monthly fees, no in-app purchases, no advertising." Our 29 SEK one-time Remove Ads beats that. The risk is not the model, it is hostile ad placement around it (see "do NOT copy"). No subscription, no rented content.
2. **The single biggest retention gap vs. the benchmarks is a "readiness score."** German apps (Migrando/Buerger-Test) and Körkort AI both lead with a computed "are you ready to pass yet?" number. We have `home.tsx` but no synthesized readiness signal — this is the highest-leverage feature add.
3. **Spaced repetition should be a simplified Leitner queue, not FSRS.** AnkiDroid/FSRS proves the value of "review what you're about to forget," but full FSRS is overkill for a finite MCQ bank. A 5-box Leitner scheduler driven off `mistakes.tsx` data gives 80% of the benefit at S/M effort.
4. **Mock-exam realism is shallow vs. BritPass and the TSO Official app.** They ship a *library* of pre-built full-format mocks plus a strict timer and a post-exam topic breakdown. Our `exam.tsx` should match the official format exactly and end on a diagnostic, not just a score.
5. **The Notion-calm design is a genuine differentiator** — most körkort/Einbürgerungstest apps are cluttered and ad-noisy. Borrow Duolingo's *feedback juice* and Quizlet's *minimal focus*, but keep the warm-white restraint. Add motion and reward signals without adding visual density.

## (c) Explicitly do NOT copy

- **Duolingo's guilt/passive-aggressive notifications** ("These reminders don't seem to work…"). Use neutral, opt-in, low-frequency reminders only. No shaming copy on a broken streak.
- **Quizlet's feature gating of Learn/Test behind a paywall** (≈5 Learn rounds, 1 test per set on free). Our adaptive drill, mocks, explanations and SR are core content and must stay free — the only paid thing is removing ads.
- **AnkiDroid's parameter-wall onboarding** (FSRS weights, retention targets, deck options exposed up front). Keep SR completely invisible; the user never sees a box number or interval setting.
- **Aggressive interstitial/rewarded ads between every question** (endemic in low-quality körkort/Einbürgerungstest apps). Ads, if shown at all to free users, go only at natural session boundaries — never mid-quiz, never mid-mock, never a forced video to see an explanation.
- **Subscriptions or rented content of any kind.** No "Plus tier," no locking newer question sets behind recurring payment. The one-time 29 SEK unlock is the entire monetization surface.
- **Fake urgency / countdown discounts on the Remove Ads purchase.** State the price plainly, once.
- **Streak mechanics that punish.** No losing XP, no "you lost your 40-day streak" full-screen interstitial. Streak Freeze auto-applies silently.
- **Visual clutter to look "feature-rich"** (badges, banners, leaderboards everywhere). Keep the Notion-calm restraint; gamification is an accent, not the layout.
