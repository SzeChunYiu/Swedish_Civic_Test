# Arabic Sample Corpus Notes

Locale: Modern Standard Arabic for Arabic-speaking Sweden newcomers.

## Register target

Formal plain MSA public-service prose. Keep sentences clear and avoid colloquial dialect in legal/civic copy.

## Seed sources

| Quality | Source | URL | Why use |
|---|---|---|---|
| official-fact+register | Informationsverige Arabic rights and obligations | https://www.informationsverige.se/ar/om-sverige/individens-rattigheter-och-skyldigheter/ | Arabic civic-orientation register for rights, duties, discrimination, norms. |
| official-fact+register | Informationsverige Arabic child rights | https://www.informationsverige.se/ar/om-sverige/individens-rattigheter-och-skyldigheter/barnets-rattigheter.html | Rights terminology and learner-facing explanations. |

## Translation risks

- Do not translate Swedish institutions into home-country institutions.
- Use MSA for app UI; localize jokes gently, not with dialect unless a future owner explicitly chooses dialectal tone.

## Reusable localization notes

- Error encouragement should reassure and teach; never shame the learner.
- Source labels must say the app is based on/reference material, not an official exam authority.
- Institution names should teach the Swedish term when the term itself matters.
