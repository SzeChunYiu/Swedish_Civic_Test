A live competitor is already in market for the Swedish citizenship test.
We must BEAT them on every axis. We CANNOT and MUST NOT access/copy their
question bank (legal + content-integrity #1 rule). We CAN learn from how
they present the product.

## Mandate (UIUX / DESIGN / CONTENT / RELEASE teams)
Study and out-execute on: landing/SEO & trust signals, onboarding flow,
free-vs-paid funnel & monetization, question/answer UX, progress &
gamification, Swedish copy tone, mobile responsiveness, accessibility,
performance. For EACH axis: (1) one-line 'how they do it', (2) our gap,
(3) a concrete shipped improvement (product-source commit) that is
demonstrably better. No copying content; emulate craft, beat substance.
